How to install GIT on MAC

Step 1
Goto below link
https://github.com/VJX369/devOps.git

Depending on your OS…the link website identifies and provides you the appropriate download on the right mid section of the screen.

Step 2 
Once following package is downloaded i.e. git-2.14.1-intel-universal-mavericks.dmg, right click on it to open with installer.

On providing your Admin credentials, a mount is created and a setup gui is pops out, do navigate with applicable options & complete the installation.

Step 3
Now open the terminal on mac and run git gui from current location…GIT GUI opens up as a result.
